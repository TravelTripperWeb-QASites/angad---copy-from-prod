about us

Ideally located in the Grand Center Arts District, AHH offers a fusion of gracious hospitality combined with socially inspiring experiences for the most discerning guests. Our first in class service, distinctive dining and bar menus and venues and wide array of amenities create a truly authentic St. Louis experience. Welcome to your personal gateway to one of the midwest’s great cities.

  • 146 rooms including 38 suites
  • First hotel that empowers guests to book rooms by the emotion of color.
  • Select accommodations with full equipped kitchenettes
  • Amenities by Molton Brown
  • Luxury linens and the most comfy beds and pillows
  • Complimentary wifi
