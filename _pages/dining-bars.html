GRAND TAVERN

by david burke

Grand Tavern by David Burke, Angad Arts Hotel’s signature restaurant is Chef Burke’s first St. Louis based concept in partnership with Esquared Hospitality. The ambience is comfortable and welcoming, serving breakfast, lunch and dinner, Sunday brunch and pre and post theatre specials. The bar and lounge features a menu designed for diners to share casual small plates and classic drinks. The main dining room showcases Chef Burke’s playful cuisine and whimsical take on classic and contemporary American dishes. Those familiar with Chef David Burke can look forward to his famous clothesline bacon and pastrami salmon. He also pays homage to St. Louis by putting his twist on local classics.

ANGAD RAINBOW TERRACE (ART)

The Angad Rainbow Terrace is an enchanting indoor/outdoor rooftop lounge, bar and event space with sweeping views including the Saint Louis city skyline and the famous Gateway Arch. Enjoy hand-crafted cocktails, new and old world wines, inspired small plate offerings created by Chef David Burke at sunset or under the moonlight.

CHAMELEON LOUNGE

Located on the hotel’s twelfth floor sky lobby, this unique lounge features the experiential environment of our giant Chameleon Lamp that immerses our guests in a 360-degree video art experience. This is the perfect magical place to socialize and enjoy world class cocktails created by renowned mixologists and an International selection of wines.

The Angad Playroom

Play your favorite board game, play your favorite instrument, order your favorite classic cocktail or a whimsically crafted one. A classic club lounge with a playfully sophisticated vibe and a modern twist. Come be playful!

24-hour room service

Whether you are a night owl, or prefer to take your morning breakfast in the comfort of your room, Chef David Burke’s unique cuisine is brought to you 24 hours a day. Call in advance or upon arrival and arrange delivery of premium spirits, wines from around the world and delicious food selections to satisfy your every craving. Allow us to curate a unique welcome or dining experience in your room or suite.
