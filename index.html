ANGAD ARTS HOTEL

Angad Arts Hotel (AAH) is a wholly new hospitality experience that embraces the visual arts, performance, fashion, literature and gastronomy. Ideally located in the heart of St. Louis’s Grand Center Arts District, AAH offers superb, gracious hospitality combined with curation of socially inspiring experiences. It is the first hotel in the world where guests are able to book their luxury accommodations not only by room type, but also by emotion of color.

Stay Colorful

Designed by the Lawrence Group especially to appeal to your emotional side, the color palette of our rooms sets the mood for your stay. Choose your room by how you are feeling in the moment. Whether your choice is passionate red, tranquility blue, a happy yellow or green rejuvenation our rooms and amenities are perfect for work and play.

Dine Distinctively

Dine at Grand Tavern, a new modern American concept from acclaimed Chef David Burke. Relax at the Chameleon Lounge or take in sweeping rooftop views of the city at Angad Rainbow Terrace while sipping hand-crafted cocktails in a space designed for sharing and socializing. Guests can also experience the flavor of Grand Tavern in the hotel’s Playroom or in the comfort of your room.

Meet Magnificently

From corporate, to the celebrated, our creative spaces are designed to fit your specific needs and offer ideal settings for both social and business events. Featured are two exquisite private events spaces, each with their own pre-function area, as well as a conference room and an outdoor rooftop garden terrace with spectacular skyline views. A wide range of exceptional food and beverage menus are available, especially imagined by Chef Burke.

Make Something New With Us

In anticipation of our opening, we invite you to join the Angad Arts Hotel (AAH) community by sharing your words, photographs and drawing to help create our online “Stay Colorful” mural.
